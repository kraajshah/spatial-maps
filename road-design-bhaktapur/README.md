## Road design — Plan & Profile (Bhaktapur, Nepal)

**Project:** Detail Engineering Survey, Design & Estimation of Pipalbot–Danda Tole–Syauli Bazaar–Moktan Tole Road Section, Bhaktapur, Nepal  
**Data:** Self-collected Total Station survey points  
**Tools:** QGIS, Google Earth Pro, Civil 3D  
**Map contents:** Plan & longitudinal profile; centerline, ROW, side drain, existing vs proposed profile; design speed 40 km/h; scale 1:1000  
**Notes:** Shared for academic demonstration. If needed, locations/data may be generalized.
